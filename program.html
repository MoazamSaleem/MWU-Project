MWU offers 48 undergraduate, 47 master, 2 specialty, and 4 PhD programs across Bale Robe Main Campus, Goba Campus, and Shashemene Campus. Programs are aligned with national development and global competitiveness.

Campus and Department-wise Programs

The following highlights are based on MWU's current academic structure and the university brief profile.

Bale Robe Main Campus

Engineering, Computing, Natural and Computational Sciences, Agriculture and Natural Resources, Business and Economics, Education and Behavioral Sciences, and Social Sciences and Humanities.

Goba Campus

Health and Medicine programs including Nursing, Public Health, Midwifery, Medicine, Pharmacy, Medical Laboratory Science, plus health-focused MSc and specialty programs.

Shashemene Campus

Nursing, Public Health, Midwifery, Accounting and Finance, Economics, Management, and Marketing programs.
