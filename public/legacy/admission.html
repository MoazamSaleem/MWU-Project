Admission Requirements

Start Your Academic Journey at MWU

Admission decisions are based on academic preparation, program eligibility, and document verification. Applicants should ensure all required materials are complete and submitted on time.

Undergraduate

  • Ethiopian Secondary School completion or recognized equivalent.
  • National entrance eligibility according to current MOE placement policy.
  • Academic transcripts and identity documents.

Graduate

  • Relevant bachelor degree from accredited institution.
  • Official transcript and degree certificate.
  • Program-specific criteria such as interviews or entrance assessment.

PhD

  • Relevant master's degree from accredited institution.
  • Research concept note aligned with departmental priorities.
  • Academic records and recommendation letters.
